Float (makes no sense)

  1. A block level element when floated, no longer takes up the whole parent. It's no longer a block. It's not even a display type. You can't add margin.
  2. The element, will go as far left (or right) as it possible can, until it runs into another floating thing or runs out of room.
  3. A floated element is in it's own universe. They dont pass their height up to non-floating parents. Static + float = weird.
    1. IMPORTANT: to force a floated element to pass it's height up to its parent... Make the parent float or Set the parent to have overflow:hidden.
